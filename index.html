OUR MISSION

Food should feel simple again.

GoodEats is being built around the idea that finding something good to eat should not feel complicated, expensive, or overwhelming. Many recipe platforms are filled with long stories, hard-to-follow steps, unrealistic ingredients, and meals that do not match how people actually cook day to day. Our mission is to create a cleaner, more practical place for people to discover meals that are quick, affordable, and realistic. Whether someone is a student, living in a dorm, cooking on a budget, or simply tired of scrolling through endless food posts with no clear direction, GoodEats is meant to make the decision easier. The goal is to organize food around what people actually care about: how long it takes, how much it costs, what tools are needed, how difficult it is, and whether it is actually worth making. As GoodEats develops, the focus will stay on clarity, usefulness, and accessibility, giving users a better way to find everyday meals without turning cooking into something stressful.

ABOUT

What is GoodEats?

GoodEats is a food discovery app focused on simple recipes, quick meals, and cheap eats that normal people actually want to make. The app is planned to help users browse meals by budget, time, difficulty, and available cooking tools, making it easier to find something that fits their situation instead of forcing them to search through random recipes.
